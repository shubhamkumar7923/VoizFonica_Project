FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS





General questions

Whether any charges have to be paid for migration from one plan to another?

No charges are payable by the subscriber for migration from one tariff plan to another tariff plan.

What are the services provided by voizfonica?

The services provided by voizfonica are prepaid,Postpaid,dongle.
Prepaid

A prepaid subscriber feels that he is being overcharged. Can he get itemized usage details in respect of his mobile connection?

Yes, if a prepaid subscriber makes a request for itemized usage details, the service provider is required to supply to him the same for a period of six months preceding the month in which the request has been made.

Can a pre-paid subscriber migrate to post-paid and vice-versa?

Yes, migration from pre-paid to post-paid and vice-versa is allowed without any migration charge and without having change in mobile number.

Can a prepaid subscriber avail any services even after his talk time is exhausted?

Services which do not affect talk time value, including incoming voice calls and SMS shall continue to be available to the prepaid subscribers during validity period even after talk time value has exhausted.

What are the payment options?

The best way to transfer amount is via debit/credit card. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment.

Can a service provider levy charge for provision of hard copy of the bill?

TRAI has prohibited levy of charges for provision of hard copy of the bill to the post-paid subscribers.

The credit limit set for a post-paid customer is to be included in monthly statement / bill.
